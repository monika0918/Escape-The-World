North America

North America is the land of adventure. Being home to Canada, United States and Mexico it offers a mixture of history, landscapes, cultures, cuisines and adventures.

Venture on Canadian wildness trek, go down the legendary road trip along Route 66 or explore the mysterious Mayan history in Mexico - there’s plenty of destinations for every taste.

Mexico

Mexico is country rich with diversity and culture. Experience your dream vacation in the Caribbean with idyllic sandy beaches, natural beauty and picturesque towns. It’s a perfect place to unwind by the crystal clear waters, explore thousands years of history with Mayan pyramids and get closer to the nature by riding a train through the mountains into the Copper Canyon.

United States

United States has variety of natural wonders like canyons, national parks and wildlife but it is also a home to some iconic cities like New York, Los Angeles or Chicago. The list of must-visits is nearly impossible to cover, the best attractions are as beautiful as they are diverse: some of the greatest canyons such as Grand, Antelope or Bryce Canyons to list a few, the Pacific Coast Highway in California, the hidden beaches of Hawaii or world-class museums all across the country.

Canada

The second largest country in the world, Canada has no shortage of beautiful landscapes and unique sites for travelers to explore. In Western Canada, the Rocky Mountains and cities of Vancouver, Victoria, and Calgary dominate most itineraries. In Central Canada, Niagara Falls, Toronto, Ottawa, Montreal, and Quebec City, are some of the most popular destinations.
